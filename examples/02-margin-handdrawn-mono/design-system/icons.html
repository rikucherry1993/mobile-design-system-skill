Icons

Token-driven inline SVG sprite, heavier 2.1 stroke to match the hand-drawn outline look. Use with <svg class="ico"><use href="#i-book"/></svg>. Never emoji.

Illustrations

The signature — hand-drawn line illustrations, monochrome, stroke:currentColor so they invert on the ink card and re-theme with everything. Use them as headers, empty states, and the concept hero.
